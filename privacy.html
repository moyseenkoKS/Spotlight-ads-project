Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

This application is designed to generate automated reports for advertising performance and integrate data into Looker Studio dashboards.

Data We Access

This application uses Google Ads API to access advertising data from authorized accounts. The data includes:

  • Campaign names and IDs
  • Ad group and ad identifiers
  • Performance metrics such as impressions, clicks, cost, and conversions

We do NOT access or process any personally identifiable information (PII).

How We Use Data

  • Generating performance reports
  • Analyzing advertising campaigns
  • Visualizing data in Looker Studio

Data Storage

We do not store Google Ads data permanently unless explicitly configured. Data may be processed temporarily for reporting purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data and securely handle API credentials.

User Control

You can revoke access to your Google Ads account at any time via your Google account settings.
