Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

This application is designed to generate automated reports for advertising performance and integrate data into Looker Studio dashboards.

Data We Access

This application uses Google Ads API to access advertising data from authorized Google Ads accounts. The data accessed includes:

  • Campaign names and IDs
  • Ad group and ad identifiers
  • Performance metrics such as impressions, clicks, cost, and conversions

We do NOT access or process any personally identifiable information (PII) of end users.

How We Use Data

The data is used solely for:

  • Generating performance reports
  • Analyzing advertising campaigns
  • Visualizing data in Looker Studio dashboards

Data Storage

We do not store Google Ads data permanently unless explicitly configured by the user. Data may be processed temporarily to generate reports.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties. Data is only used within the application for reporting purposes.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect access to your data, including secure handling of API credentials.

User Control

Users can revoke access to their Google Ads account at any time via their Google account permissions.
